About

Biology meets
production engineering.

I work at the intersection of biology, data engineering and software — turning complex biological data into reliable, reusable workflows that R&D teams can depend on day to day.

With a Master's in Bioinformatics (Platform Engineering track) and 2+ years of hands-on industry experience, I specialise in industrialising spatial transcriptomics analyses and integrating imaging with omics data in multi-modal frameworks.

Everything I build is designed with real industry constraints in mind: scalable architectures, clear separation of data/code/config, and documentation written for stakeholders beyond the academic world.

Target sectors: Biotech & pharma R&D, MedTech & diagnostics, spatial biology, digital pathology, and health data platforms.
